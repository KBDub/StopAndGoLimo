## Rule

Em dashes (—) are not permitted anywhere in website copy. This includes headings, subheadings, body copy, hero descriptions, meta descriptions, alt text, structured data, and any documentation that becomes site content.

**Why:** The client explicitly banned em dashes across all copy. The preference is for plain, readable punctuation that reads naturally at a 7th grade level.

**How to apply:**
- Replace "Book Fast — Spots Fill Quickly" with "Book Fast, Spots Fill Quickly."
- Replace "from pickup — to drop-off" with "from pickup to drop-off."
- Replace "around the clock — 365 days a year" with "around the clock, 365 days a year."
- When an em dash separates two independent clauses, break into two sentences or use a comma.
- Double hyphens (--) are also banned. Same rule.
