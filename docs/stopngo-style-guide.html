03 — Hierarchy

Color Hierarchy

The order in which colors carry weight across the experience.

1

Midnight Navy #15162C

Foundation color — dominant backgrounds, headers, and the footer. Sets the luxury tone.

2

Champagne Gold #DCB57E

Primary brand color — CTAs, accents, dividers, and every interactive hover state.

3

White #FFFFFF

Container color — cards, primary text on navy, and high-contrast surfaces.

4

Cloud Grey #E8E9EC

Light surface color — alternating sections and breathing room in light mode.

5

Slate #69727D

Secondary text color — captions, metadata, and muted supporting copy.

6

Signal Gold #FEC42D

Accent color — used sparingly for "24/7" badges and high-attention highlights only.
